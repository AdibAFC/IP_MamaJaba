Our Story

Welcome to MamaJaba?, your number one source for safe, reliable, and convenient rickshaw rides. We’re dedicated to providing you the very best ride-hailing service, with an emphasis on efficiency, safety, and affordability.

Founded in 2024, MamaJaba? has come a long way from its beginnings in Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology(CUET).When we first started out, our passion for making urban transportation hassle-free drove us to start this initiative, so that MamaJaba? can offer you the most dependable rickshaw ride experience. We now serve customers all over the cuet areas and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own service.

Our Mission

At MamaJaba?, our mission is to revolutionize urban transportation by providing a seamless, efficient, and sustainable way to get around the city. We aim to connect riders with drivers in a way that benefits both, ensuring quick, safe, and cost-effective travel.

What We Do

MamaJaba? connects riders with trusted rickshaw drivers via our user-friendly website. Whether commuting, running errands, or exploring the city, we ensure a smooth journey with services like:

  • Ride-Hailing: Request a ride easily.
  • Driver Ratings: Rate drivers and read reviews for quality assurance.
  • Enhanced Security: Prioritizing your safety
